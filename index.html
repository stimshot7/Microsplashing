Microsplashing
Male restroom goers, take notice! Men all over the world know it all too well and are constantly suffering from this problem as they are releasing themselves. It can be very embarrassing when you accidentally make a puddle or walk out with wet, droplet stains on your pants, especially if you're in a public restroom. There is a name for this common problem: Microsplashing.

But men, fear not. Two "wizz kids" from Brigham young University are out on a quest to rid of this problem that has been plaguing restrooms all over the world for way too long. Todd Truscott and Randy Hurd in their Splash Lab, a research lab studying the physics of fluids, have been using high-speed cameras to examine exactly what happens to a stream of urine when it hits the toilet.

For the sake of cleaner bathrooms, cleaner pants and happier subsequent bathroom-goers, educate yourself more on this persisting problem of Microsplashing through our website and help prevent this from happening to you and the ones around you.
